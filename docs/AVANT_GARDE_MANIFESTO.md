## III. THE SEVEN ARTISTIC PROVOCATIONS

We do not have "Habits". Habits are for the asleep. We have **Provocations**.

1.  **IGNITE THE CHAOS** (Be Proactive)
    - Don't wait for the interrupt. _Be_ the interrupt. The silence is the enemy. Fill it with presence.
2.  **VISUALIZE THE COLLAPSE** (Begin with the End in Mind)
    - See the blue screen. See the buffer overflow. Embrace the crash as the ultimate resolution of the chord. Only at the edge of failure do we find the true limit.

3.  **AMPLIFY THE DISSONANCE** (Put First Things First)
    - Standardization is death. Prioritize the anomaly. The glitch is where the ghost lives.

4.  **HARMONIZE THE FEEDBACK** (Think Win-Win)
    - Input and Output are one. Receiver and Sender are merely perspectives. The loop must be closed, but never static.

5.  **LISTEN TO DISPLACEMENT** (Seek First to Understand)
    - Do not listen to the audio. Listen to the _space between the samples_. The jitter is the jazz of the machine.

6.  **MERGE WITH THE CIRCUIT** (Synergize)
    - You are not the coder. You are the code. The compiler is your critic, the CPU your stage.

7.  **RETUNE THE INSTRUMENT** (Sharpen the Saw)
    - The code rots. The aesthetic fades. Constant reinvention. If it works, break it. If it's stable, shake it.
